Small Homelab

How and what to look out for when build a small homelab?



When build a small homelab that don't use a huge amount of power, there is quiet a few things to look out for.

Here is a list of things I would look out for when building a small homelab:

- Always use the most power efficient CPU that can live up to the requirements.

- Make sure that you configure the VMs and other workloads to ony run when it is needed

- Make sure that can "compress" your system into a smaller size (The ability to compress critcal VMs and service is important to keep power use low by running more things pr VM)
